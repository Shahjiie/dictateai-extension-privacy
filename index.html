Privacy Policy — DictateAI

Last updated: March 25, 2026

Data Collection

DictateAI does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Speech Recognition

All speech recognition is performed locally in your browser using the built-in Web Speech API. Audio is processed by your browser's speech engine and is never sent to any third-party server by this extension.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

Changes

If this policy changes, the update will be reflected in this file and in the extension's version history.
